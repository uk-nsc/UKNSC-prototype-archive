UK NSC meetings and minutes

Details of upcoming UK National Screening Committee (UK NSC) meetings, plus minutes from past meetings.

From:
UK National Screening Committee
Published
3 June 2021

The UK NSC holds 3 meetings a year to make recommendations on screening programmes. Meetings are usually in February, June and October. They are held in London twice a year, with the June meeting hosted by one of other 3 UK countries on a rotating basis.

Meetings are due to be held on:

  • Friday 25 June 2021
  • Thursday 4 November 2021

The UK NSC will usually publish draft minutes and a summary of recommendations 6 weeks after the meeting.

For minutes from before 2015 please see the UK government web archive.
