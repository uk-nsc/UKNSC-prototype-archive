Adult screening programme Anaemia

Anaemia is a condition caused by a lack of red blood cells, resulting in weakness, breathlessness and reduced energy. It is quite common during pregnancy when a woman needs more iron and other vitamins.

Read more on NHS UK

UK NSC's screening recommendation The last UK NSC review of this condition occurred in July 2006.

Screening is not currently recommended for this condition

Guidelines covered by NICE

All pregnant women should be offered a test for anaemia. Read more about NICE recommendation.
