The role of the UK National Screening Committee

The UK National Screening Committee (UK NSC):

  • advises ministers and the NHS in England, Northern Ireland, Scotland and Wales about which conditions and diseases to screen for
  • supports the implementation and improvement of NHS population screening programmes in all 4 UK countries

We review conditions against evidence review criteria to determine if a screening programme is effective, viable and appropriate.

You can read more about the evidence review process, and see a complete list of UK NSC recommendations (listed by condition). View the UK NSCâ€™s code of practice.
