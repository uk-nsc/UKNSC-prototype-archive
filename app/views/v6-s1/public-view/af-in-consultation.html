Recommendation

Screening is not currently recommended for this condition

Key findings supporting the UK NSC recommendation:

  • the review completed in 2014 found that it is not clear that those identified as at risk through screening would benefit from early diagnosis
  • the evidence review also highlighted concerns over the available test and the quality of current treatment pathways for AF
  • the Committee identified a need to improve clinical management and standardise the treatment services currently available to those with diagnosed AF

Atrial Fibrillation

Atrial Fibrillation causes a fast and erratic heart beat. It is a complication of various diseases. Medication can slow the heart rate back to normal, and ease symptoms. In some cases, treatment can restore the heart back to a normal rhythm.
