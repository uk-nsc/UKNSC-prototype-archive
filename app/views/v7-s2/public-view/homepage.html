What the UK National Screening Committee does

The UK National Screening Committee (UK NSC) provides advice in England, Northern Ireland, Wales and Scotland about which conditions and diseases to screen for and supports the current NHS population screening programmes.

We regularly review conditions against criteria to determine if a screening programme is viable, effective and appropriate. Read more about the process we use to review evidence.

View our complete list of UK NSC recommendations on NHS Population screening

As a member of the public you can submit your comments on conditions that have affected you or your loved ones. View conditions that are open for public comments
